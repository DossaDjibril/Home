NBA teams

There are 30 teams in the NBA that are split up into conferences. Each conference has 15 teams, there is an eastern conference and a western conference. Each conference has 3 divisions each. There are 5 different teams in every division. The 6 divisions are the atlantic division, the central division, the southeast division, the Northwest division, the Pacific division, and the Southwest division.
