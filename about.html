About the NBA

The NBA also known as the National Basketball Associaton is one of the many basketball leagues in the world. It is the most popular basketball league where the best players from around the world play. The average NBA player makes around 10 million dollars a year and the highest payed NBA player makes around 48 million dollars a year.

The NBA manages everything that happens throughout the league like the marketing, referee operations, finances, data collection and stats, they also handle what trades happen. When a trade is created between two teams and they both agree on it. It is sent to the NBA to look over and approve. The NBA makes around 10 billion US dollars a year. The Commissioner of the NBA is Adam Silver. His role is to be the ultimate decision maker for everything that happens in the league and is in charge of making policies and rules that govern the league.
